First Aid Information

CPR Instructions

1. Check for responsiveness

2. Call for help (911)

3. Begin chest compressions (100-120 per minute)

4. Give rescue breaths if trained

For Burns

1. Cool the burn with cool (not cold) running water

2. Remove jewelry or tight clothing

3. Cover loosely with sterile bandage

4. Take pain relievers if needed
